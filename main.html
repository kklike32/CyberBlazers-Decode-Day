Two years ago, the cousin of one of our teammates had gotten into a car accident resulting in her death, and with the buzz of self-driving cars circulating the community, we decided to create a simulation of a self driving car.

We are a team of high schoolers from Dublin High School focusing on coding solutions that applies to our everyday lives. We are highly motivated to secure a job in the computer science field and believe that we have the abilities and substantial support of teachers and parents to purse this career. Some of the abilities which have helped us are having leadership skills, being a team player, and an ambitious learner.
